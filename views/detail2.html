SpiderMan: Far from Home

2019 Action 2h 9m
Following the events of Avengers: Endgame (2019), Spider-Man must step up to take on new threats in a world that has changed forever.In Ixtenco, Mexico, Nick Fury and Maria Hill investigate an unnatural storm and encounter the Earth Elemental. Quentin Beck, a super-powered individual, arrives to defeat the creature. Beck subsequently defeats the Air Elemental and is recruited by Fury and Hill. Meanwhile, in New York City, the Midtown School of Science and Technology completes its year, which was restarted to accommodate the students who previously disintegrated five years ago as a result of Thanos' actions.
Cast: Tom Holland, Samuel L. Jackson, Jake Gyllenhaal
Director: Jon Watts
